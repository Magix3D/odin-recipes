Quesabirria Tacos

Cook's Note:

This is a classic Mexican Quesabirria tacos recipe that's perfect for a delicious meal!

Ingredients:

  • 500g beef chuck, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 200g shredded cheese (like mozzarella or Oaxaca)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, cook the beef chuck over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the canned tomatoes, chili powder, and cumin. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve in warm tortillas with shredded cheese and enjoy!
