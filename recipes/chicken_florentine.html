Chicken Florentine

Cook's Note:

This is a classic Chicken Florentine recipe that's perfect for a delicious meal!

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 200g shredded cheese (like mozzarella or Oaxaca)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, cook the chicken breast over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the canned tomatoes, chili powder, and cumin. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve with cooked pasta and enjoy!
