Bolognese

Cook's Note:

This is a classic Italian Bolognese sauce that's perfect for pasta!

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve over cooked pasta and enjoy!
